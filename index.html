Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my caffeine intake?

Open the app and use the "Log Consumption" button on any coffee detail page, or use the Quick Log feature from Control Center.

How do I add a custom coffee?

Go to the Profile tab, scroll to "My Coffees" section, and tap "Add Custom Coffee". Fill in the recipe details, taste profile, and brewing steps.

How do I set up reminders?

Navigate to the Reminders tab, tap the "+" button, select a coffee, and configure your reminder schedule.

How do I enable widgets?

Long press on your Home Screen, tap the "+" button, search for "CoffeeAtlas", and select the "Caffeine Tracker" widget.

How do I use Siri Shortcuts?

Say "Hey Siri, get my caffeine in CoffeeAtlas" or "Hey Siri, log coffee in CoffeeAtlas". You can also create custom shortcuts in the Shortcuts app.

How do I change the language?

Go to Profile > Language and select your preferred language from the list.

How do I change my daily caffeine limit?

The app automatically calculates your recommended limit based on your age range in Profile settings. You can adjust your age range to change the limit.
