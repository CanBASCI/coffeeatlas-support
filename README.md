## About CoffeeAtlas

CoffeeAtlas is your complete companion for exploring, tracking, and mastering the art of coffee brewing. Discover 50+ coffee recipes, track your daily caffeine intake, and brew the perfect cup with step-by-step guides and built-in timers.

## Features

- 50+ coffee recipes from around the world
- Smart caffeine tracking with personalized limits
- Interactive brewing guides with timers
- Custom coffee creation
- Smart reminders
- Home Screen widgets
- Siri Shortcuts integration
- Live Activities & Dynamic Island support
- Multi-language support (6 languages)
- Comprehensive analytics

## Getting Help

For support, questions, or to report issues, please see [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) or [create an issue](https://github.com/CanBASCI/coffeeatlas-support/issues).

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection required. Your privacy is protected.
